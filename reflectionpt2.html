My Reflection on the 2nd Quarter

During the second quarter of our TLE-ICT class, I experienced a lot of ups and downs. Simply because I have a hard time truly understanding the lessons. However, when we were taking our tests and recitation about the lessons that were discussed, I suddenly remember everything that I have studied and what was taught to us. It was really a miracle, or maybe I just don't have enough self-confidence when it comes to academics. Eitherway, I'm truly grateful to the people who helped me during this quarter, and to Ms. Uminga too. I just think that I wasn't able to truly give my best during this quarter because of all the back-to-back activities, tests, and performance tasks that our teachers constantly give us. However, that didn't stop me from still trying hard to pass this class.

The quarter wasn't without its struggles, but I pushed through, learning how to manage my time better and realizing the importance of teamwork and persistence. It may have been difficult, but I know it'll all be worth it in the end. I look forward to applying these lessons in the next quarter.
